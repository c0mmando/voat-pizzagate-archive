Key here is to add: site:voat.co/v/pizzagate after your search terms, this tells Google to search for your search terms within the specified domain after site:

Google will also index: Deleted threads. comments and full URLs (perfect for checking if a link has been posted already)

how-to http://i.imgur.com/oai8Wu6.png

Step 1: go to www.google.com

Step 2: add your search terms/url

Step 3: add site:voat.co/v/pizzagate

Step 4: hit search and enjoy
