TL;DR: Did find a perfect solution but it's not finished yet, WE NEED A BLOCKCHAIN BASED SOLUTION in which we are allowed to take part of the network (Make this your requirement)

Since posts and files are being removed we, as a community, are looking for a way to backup and store our files permanently.

People did good research and came with a solution called IPFS.pics

IPFS is a p2p filesystem, so it's easy to download files from eachother, a distributed network.

The problem with this solution is that the files do not exist in the network until somebody requests them. So if somebody decides to download your file, it requests the hash and sends the file to you thus become more decentrilzed.

THIS IS NOT WHAT WE ARE LOOKING FOR

Attackers are able to disable the internet of the node before decentrilization, in this case the files are not available anymore.

THAT'S WHY THE PPL OF IPFS.pics DID ANOTHER PROJECT!

http://filecoin.io/

FileCoin, it's blockchain based, based on bitcoin and is permanent.

Files are always in the network.

I'd recommend researching this because I did not have the time do dive in depth yet. But this is what we are looking for.

Please start the discussion because this is important for the future of our investigation
