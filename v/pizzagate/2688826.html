The case Police began investigating after they received two reports from the Penn Dept of H. S. ChildLine that referenced a sexual assault that occurred in July 2014. The director wrote an incident report, but could not find it for police. Richardson said first that he could not recall an incident report being generated, then didn't think there was one, documents state.

Police interviewed Richardson a second time on Feb. 23 and asked him if he had located an incident report about the alleged abuse. Richardson said he had not found one.
