Titles can now be edited within 10 minutes of original submission. Thanks @PuttItOut! ( pizzagate )

submitted 2018-10-18T00:53:11 by Vindicator

Putt and testers have been working hard to add some additional features to Voat, and one of them is the ability to edit titles within 10 minutes of submission. @Peaceseeker talks about it here :

A title being editted behaves in the same way as a title-in-formulation: Unicode will get stripped, character limits of more than 10 and less than 200 are imposed, and trying to get around these limits or introduce unicode during editting does nothing.

10 minute timer seems to work. Clicking "edit" before the timer runs out and hitting "submit" after the timer runs out sends the user to a goat page. Before the goat loaded I saw the words "Whoopsy Daisy" as clickable. Probably a text form of the goat before loading.

Besides the fact that an infinite amount of edits seem to be allowable during that ten minute time span (which might be abusable by malicious bots?) I can find nothing wrong with this feature at all. Great idea, and great work Putt, people will really enjoy this ability.
