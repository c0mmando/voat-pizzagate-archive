Hi everyone,

I read a couple of articles recently about Chrissy Teigen and company disabling their twitter after being exposed for pizzagate-related material. While screenshots of tweets are better than nothing, it is better to have a third-party archive of the tweets. That way, people can't say they were photoshopped. If the tweets in question were deleted within the past 3-6 months, you may be able to recover them from a search engine that has a cache feature. To my knowledge, three that have this feature are google, bing, and yandex. I will give the instructions for google, but most search engines will operate similarly. When you go to one of the search engines, you can find all of the tweets for that particular account by typing in "site:" and then the twitter URL. (e.g. site:https://twitter.com/chrissyteigen) Also, check the mobile URL as some tweets may have only been cached with that version too. (e.g. site:https://mobile.twitter.com/chrissyteigen/) If you are looking for a specific tweet, you can put the search term in front. (e.g. pedo site:https://twitter.com/chrissyteigen) On the search results, it will include all of the old tweets. In order to open the old tweets, you will have to click on the down arrow to the right of the URL and right-click on cached. From there you will need to paste that cached URL into archive.is and the tweet will be archived permanently. Below are some of the tweets I archived for Chrissy Teigen.
