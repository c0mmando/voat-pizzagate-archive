If you don't like some of the names I have here, you can simply ignore the results which include them.

Methodology

I went to each account and scrolled down until all follwers were in view, and then text copied them, and then processed the text to extract all the usernames. Then I ran a program I wrote to find the follows the above people had in common and group them.

I chose to take follows because that was the purest form of connection between them, the expression of interest in what another user has to say.

Considerations:

Obviously, it should be noted that following something is not deterministic of involvement.

Also, there were some accounts that I was interested in (such as outspoken Clinton supporter and spirit cooker Lady Gaga) who had too many follows to include. Others I was interested in, discussed here on voat, but could find no account.

Finally, it is understood that these users often have administrators manning their accounts.
