Ok, shit is getting a bit fuckn fruity in these threads.

Im not seeking to impinge freedom of speech, but maybe we can set up a 'Pizzagate Speculative' subfolder or something for folks to thrash out their more conjecture based discussions and theories

IMHO, this primary pizzagate folder should focus on the more clinically investigative material - not saying it should refrain from conjecture - that's part of an investigation , thats how the imagination comes into play for an investigator to detect what might be a lead worth pursuing.

But if you're gonna start throwing in hollow earth antarctica cthulhu crossdressing werewolves and shit, stop to reconsider your own psyche from this perspective - which may or may not have merit in your personal case:

Most people feel the need to feel important. Its part of the function that makes us seek to excel, its a valid human trait. Some people, of a more uncommon bent, find their sense of personal importance from distancing themselves as far as possible from the average ignorant slumbering joe. This too is valid and important, it sets up in the human social pool people who can think outside the box. They may not be the best day-to-day players in the tribe but when exogenous circumstances strike and the common pleb is dumbfounded, some of these 'differentiated' people can really step up to the plate. Your very participation in voat (shills apart) signify you as this type of person. BUT - there are character flaws common (not universal) to we differentiated people.

One common character flaw is to go too far out on a limb to persuade self or others of your 'special access to arcane knowledge no-one else knows or is smart enough to figure out' Its a contrarian ego trap

resist it.

Indulge it if you must - but elsewhere, on other forums. Our work here must be credible, our analysis should be dry enough that if an average Joe-cop gets curious about 'all this pizzagate shit' and stumbles on this website that he will be IMPRESSED by the imaginative RESTRAINT in terms of evidence and become an ally/convert

is that cool?
