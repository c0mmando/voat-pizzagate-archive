h2d 2016-12-20T10:05:14

If you're on iOS, screenshot the pics or long-press on them, save them to your photo gallery. Alternatively, best option is to have the picture open in a new tab if you're in safari or another browser, use the "share" or action buton at the bottom bar on the screen (the one that looks like a vertical rectangle/slightly off square with an arrow pointing upwards in the center going out of the top - like this [ ^ ] ) when that brings up the actionable menu, then you can choose to save the photo. It helps if you save the photos directly because in the case of anything potentially evidentiary, it preserves possible EXIF data and encoded information that would be scrubbed otherwise or potentially mislabeled and linked back to your device if you screenshot it. Once you save the photos you can upload them to sl.i.mg or make a gallery on slimg or imgur and then archive it on archive.is for posterity if you want, that's probably easiest.
