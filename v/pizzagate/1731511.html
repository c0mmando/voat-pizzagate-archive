I'm not so sure what exactly "the ratios" is describing.

So, cannibidol is a cannabinoid , or a naturally occurring chemical found in marijuana plants (along with THC, as you described it). These cannabinoids act on cannabanoid receptors located throughout the body which makes up the endocannabinoid system . When learning about the endocannabinoid system (ECS), you'll find that it deals with things such as your appetite, mood, memory, and sense of pain. The ECS also regulates the psychoactive effect of the drug.

I believe you're describing the ratios of different CBD, THC, and other cannabinoids produced in the different strains grown.

Cheap pot doesn't get you all that high. The really good stuff is most likely the strains grown in extremely controlled environments and also most likely the strains grown with the most potent levels of these cannabinoids. They're the strains that will have high levels of different cannabinoids present, and they're the ones that will get you more stoned.
