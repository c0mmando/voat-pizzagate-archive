They are not literally talking about cards

Showing cards is a poker metaphor, it simply means revealing what you have. When someone calls your bet on the final turn you have to show your cards. If the fold, you don't have to show your cards, but sometimes as a strategy a player will choose to show his card, to set up another play down the line. If I show him now, I have the ace, maybe next when I'm on a bluff he'll fold to me thinking I have the ace.
