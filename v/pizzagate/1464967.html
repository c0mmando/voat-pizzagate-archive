If you see a thread debunking a piece of evidence AND THE THREAD SUFFICIENTLY DEBUNKS THE EVIDENCE 1. upvote that thread. We must weed out bad evidence (but don't delete it)

Any other ideas? Add onto what I've written or change it., but let's try and get a consensus on how we should operate.
