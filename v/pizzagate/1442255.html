Fly-posting can certainly be a tricky area, so my best advice here would be to go into pubs, cafes and the likes, dumping a load of small leaflets on tables without even bothering to ask if it's okay. The problem here is that it'll take a lot of prints - 4 per sheet of A4 means 400 leaflets would cost at least Â£5 here, probably more, and a lot of those are going to be binned before people get to read them. Thus, they need to be handed out if anything. As for posters, my advice there would be to tape leaflets to cash machines, that way people are at least forced to look at them. In England they have the power to disperse and in this case I don't reckon they'd allow such a march and would therefore exercise that power if one went ahead.
