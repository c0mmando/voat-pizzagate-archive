Dear community, if I gather such keyword clouds a couple of times a month (or when a focus of our investigations have remarkable changes), I'd like to ask in advance should these be new posts or should I just edit this one https://voat.co/v/pizzagate/1776803 Personally, I find the following pros and cons:

If posted as new

  • pro: Gathers visibility, helpful especially for new users to find our current focus of investigations
  • pro: Having its own upvoats and downvoats, helpful for myself to follow the interest towards this work
  • con: A new post always pollutes the forum, so it definitely shouldn't be too often

If editing into one post

  • pro: Helpful if using bookmark, having one place for all the info
  • con: Lacks visibility, probably making this work not worth it over time
  • con: The post might get too long - at least a text form of the keywords must be dropped, leaving only images (like above)

Have your say, I'll do what the community sees as the best option.
