What are reborn dolls?

Reborn baby dolls started to show up around the world in the 1990's, with doll artists wanting a more realistic looking baby doll.

A Reborn Doll is a vinyl baby doll kit that has been enhanced using paints and real hair fibers to resemble a human baby. The process of creating a reborn doll is referred to as Reborning, and the doll artists are referred to as Reborners.
