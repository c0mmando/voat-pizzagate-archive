Hi All,

This is a follow up to this thread here: https://www.voat.co/v/pizzagate/2245339 . In this Elsagate themed thread, it was discussed how several youtube videos had the comments removed so researchers could no longer try to descipher the code. While I have yet to figure out a way to archive youtube video comments, I did find that the **channel ** comments could be archived. If the channel has a discussion page, (e.g. https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCenMWMQOjul4ufo49xkvTYg/discussion http://archive.is/Tc2U1 ) it can be archived and the comments will pick up. Unfortunately, some channels do not have this feature on them.

I hope this helps even if just a little. Let me know if any of you find a way to archive video comments.
