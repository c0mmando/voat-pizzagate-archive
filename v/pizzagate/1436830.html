We already know the connection between Spirit Cooking at the Podestas.

Spirit Cooking was the name of an old performance art piece by Marina. About 30 years later she did a kickstarter campaign to fund her new institute, and made reference to "Spirit Cooking" in the reward offering for the $10,000 level donations, inviting people over to her house for dinner and soup-making. Calling it "Spirit cooking" is a pun.

Podesta is a fan and collector and donated to her kickstarter. It's no more complicated than that.
