Submission Rules - Removal tier

  • 1. Do not link to CP
  • 2. Posts not about PIZZAGATE specifically will be removed.
  • 3. EVERY new claim needs to cite at least one source and provide an explanation as to why you believe it is relevant or furthers the investigation
  • 4. VIDEO post titles need to reflect an accurate description of the actual content. If you can't provide an accurate TL;DR in the title then you MUST submit it as a discussion post.

Submission Quality Rules - Flair tier

We need to make our information easy to connect, consume and understand. To this end posts need to:

  • 5. Have descriptive titles that explain the content of the post in an intuitive way
  • 6. Where the title is not sufficient to explain the connections/content of a link, the body must explain the connection
  • 7. Discussion posts must have sources and coherently explain points

The main intent is to make information easily consumable and help people make connections. Bring this intent to every post you make.

On Concerns

  • 8. Meta concerns are important but we will keep them to a single thread to avoid diluting the sub. All other meta concern posts will be removed.

Here is the current concern thread: https://voat.co/v/pizzagate/1467885 - PLEASE ping ( @username ) the mods so we can get a notification and address your concerns!

Zero tolerance for spam

  • 9. Spam posts will be deleted and repeat offenders banned.

Anonymity

Everyone is well aware of how dangerous this area of research is. Anyone asking for real info on anyone else for the purposes of identifying them (or anything that looks like this) should be ignored and downvoted, and will be treated with suspicion.

  • 10. Posting a members name or links to their social media for the purposes of revealing identity will result in a deletion and ban
