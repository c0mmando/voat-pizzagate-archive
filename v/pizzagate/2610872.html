New flair policy tweak -- PLEASE Read and help out! ( pizzagate )

submitted 2018-06-28T05:12:41 by Vindicator

After a year and a half, it suddenly occurred to me that we could enable the ability to search by flair like "New Evidence" and "Swamp Takedown" at https://searchVoat.co very simply by asking submitters whose posts receive a flair to edit the body text to include the name of the flair, since the search engine scrapes the post body for keywords if you enable that option. So, from now on,

Please take a moment to edit your threads to include the name of the flair your post receives whenever you get one.

You can do it for any post that isn't archived. If anyone would care to go through NEW, TOP, and HOT and invite users who received a flair to do so in case they don't see this thread, please jump in and leave comments in their threads! :-) If we work together, we can create a flair search.

As always, please feel free to ping or message mods if you think a submission should be flaired. :-) Also, if you happen to see a flair that needs to be changed to either "Confirmed" or "Debunked", do let us know.

Thanks!
