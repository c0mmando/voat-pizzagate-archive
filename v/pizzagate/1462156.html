The trunk of the tree, is the solid information we have in regards to #pizzagate. The branches are connections we have made because of previously uncovered information, such as the money laundering discovery that is currently stickied. It is our responsibility to self moderate, and to make sure that we maintain focus, and don't allow ourselves to self destruct by allowing people to post theories, or claims that have no evidence, or very flimsy evidence.
