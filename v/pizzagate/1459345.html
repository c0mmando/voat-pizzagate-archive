Houston: we have a problem. Multiple duplicate threads. ( pizzagate )

submitted 2016-12-04T00:42:08 by Melitica

We are all here to combine efforts in discovering and uncovering crimes related to pizzagate - child porn is only the tip of the iceberg which leads to money laundering, public manipulation, arms dealing, child trafficking and potentially, treason.

That said, when a new thread is started on an old topic, all of our efforts are diluted as people struggle to reach "aha" moments that have already been reached.

Please, please, please make an attempt to read beyond the first page to check for an existing thread before starting a new one.

In addition, when titling, please limit your use of the words - bombshell, shocking, smoking gun...and phrases like - this is it, I have found it, OMG...or similar. The draw and attraction that is provided momentarily will be lost as soon as the next "shocking" revelation thread is started.

If you care to comment, I would like help in identifying duplicate content threads and welcome your ideas about how we can avoid duplication of threads and efforts.
