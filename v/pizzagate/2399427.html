FAMOUS JACKSON WHITE MAGNOLIA TREE CUT DOWN AFTER CHRISTMAS

I would be remiss if I didn't mention one other connection with white magnolia which has been in the news. On Dec. 26, 2017, while the First Family was on vacation at Mar-a-Lago, the historic but ailing white magnolia tree on the south side of the White House was severely cut back on orders by Melania Trump.

This iconic tree which is featured on the back of the $20 bill, was planted by Andrew Jackson as a tribute to his wife who died after his election and has been in the background of many historic occasions. Interestingly, This CNN article features a strange image of Barbara Bush and Hillary Clinton sharing a moment in front of the Jackson Magnolia on the occasion of Hillary's first tour of the White House: https://cdn.cnn.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/171225155143-magnolia---1-exlarge-169.jpg

It's been suggested that Melania had the tree taken down as part of the extensive exorcism and spiritual purging of the White House. In fact, Q mysteriously announced on Dec. 19, a week before the tree's removal, that

SATAN has left the WH.

Could the removal of the Jackson Magnolia have been a symbolic gesture that the exorcism was complete? The tree was so large it covered the windows of the Presidential bedroom and the dining room and was an intimate part of life in the residence. Laura Bush considered it such an important feature of life in the White House she commissioned a set of china commemorating the tree: https://georgewbush-whitehouse.archives.gov/news/releases/2009/01/images/20090107-1_p010709sc-0260-515h.jpg

There is a smaller plate with a butterfly, bee and dragonfly: https://georgewbush-whitehouse.archives.gov/news/releases/2009/01/images/20090107-1_p010709sc-0291-540v.html

Chelsea Clinton thanked Melania for preserving part of the tree:

“Thank you to all the Chief Horticulturists and everyone from the National Park Service who have taken care of the beautiful grounds of the @WhiteHouse over the years,” tweeted Clinton, who grew up in the White House during her father Bill Clinton's two terms in office. “Thank you @FLOTUS for preserving part of a tree I & so many have treasured.”

Of course, this may all be perfectly coincidental and a nothing-burger, but the timing does seem significant.
