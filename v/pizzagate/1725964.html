Also, here's a Voat protip: If you screw up a headline, you can easily remedy it by deleting the post yourself and creating a new one. There should be a row of clickable actions right beneath the post -- next to the comment count. One should be "delete". If you can't see this, you are viewing the mobile page. Go to your browser settings pulldown menu and click the "view desktop site" box.

Another tip is to first create your post as a Direct Message to yourself. You can work on it, then send it to yourself to finish later, add links to, edit with fresh eyes, or whatever. When you want to edit it, go to the message in your inbox, click the "source" button (right next to "delete"), and copy all the text from the source window (this picks up any formatting or hyperlinks you have already added, which copypasting the finished text of the post does not). This is my elaborate work-around since Voat doesn't offer a save draft option, learned the hard way by working for several days on the protests post, adding hyperlinks, etc., only to accidentally hit the back arrow at 4am Wednesday morning and lose the whole kit-and-kaboodle.
