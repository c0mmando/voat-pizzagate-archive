How to Archive Deleted Reddit Posts and Comments ( pizzagate )

submitted 2019-03-23T04:52:17 by IShallNotFear

I came across this today and was surprised that I hadn't heard of it. If a Reddit post or comment gets deleted by a moderator, you can archive it by using ceddit.com. Ceddit.com redirects to https://snew.notabug.io/ . To archive a page, all you have to do is paste the original Reddit URL into archive.is and change the r to a c, making the URL read "ceddit" instead of "reddit." However, this will not work with Reddit posts that were removed by the author. For example, I was able to bring up the deleted comments on this post. "The Truth About Barney" https://voat.co/v/pizzagatewhatever/3042364 http://archive.is/73y4q However, I was not able to bring up the following post as the original was removed by the author. "Reddit Confession Post About Dan Schneider" https://voat.co/v/pizzagate/2481849 http://archive.is/KljpJ

It seems that the bot @derram used to do this automatically for Reddit URLs. https://www.voat.co/v/pizzagate/2928661/15707754 However, that seems to no longer be the case. As usual, I will link this post into /v/askpizzagate EDIT: Here is the link: https://voat.co/v/AskPizzagate/3114200
