Mark Lombardi (March 23, 1951 – March 22, 2000) was an American neo-conceptual artist who specialized in drawings that document alleged financial and political frauds by power brokers, and in general "the uses and abuses of power"

He is not connected to pizzagate or CGI per say, but his body of works documenting the interconnected webs of power structures seemed to me to be an excellent reference for people looking into these power structures (such as those connected with CGI), or who might be interested in his methods of documentation. The FBI and Department of homeland security were both interested in his images & research- and the FBI sought out one of his images to study after 9/11 attacks.
