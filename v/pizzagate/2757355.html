Once there, they were used in the production of child pornography and for ritual sacrifices. He said that the Hand of Death's operations were based in Texas and included trafficking in children and drugs, as well as other illegal pursuits (contract murder as well, presumably) and that the cult counted among its members socially prominent individuals, including high level politicians.
