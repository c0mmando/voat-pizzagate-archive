In October's plea agreement, Salling acknowledged that he downloaded the images from the internet in 2015 and used software designed to conceal his activity

Salling's charge carries a maximum 20-year prison sentence, but under the plea agreement the parties agreed that a four to seven-year prison sentence would be appropriate
