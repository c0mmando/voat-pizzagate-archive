Because it prominently displays the boy lover logo and other disturbing symbolism to a public park in a city known for an historic and ongoing human trafficking network.

The place is June Key Delta Community Center in Portland, Oregon. A red painted metal sculpture depicts the figure of an adult female forming a heart symbol with her hands. A crack runs through the center of her body, splitting it in two, while conjoining it at the heart with a triangle within a triangle, designed by utilizing the negative space of the crack. The triangle is recognizably the boy lover symbol. The heart also connects to the girl lover symbol. The crack may be interpreted however you wish.
