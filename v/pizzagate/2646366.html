The link does work, it just has to be copied and pasted.

Here's a trick so people can click on it and it will work:

Put a title for the link in brackets. Immediately follow that with the link in parentheses.

For example, this, but without the space between the second bracket and the first parenthesis:

[Fiona Barnett's Statement] ( https://steemit.com/child/ @thesparkafilm/fiona)

Just copy and paste the line above into your new thread, and eliminate the space between the second bracket and the first parenthesis. You'll end up with this, below, a working "hyperlink":

Fiona Barnett's Statement
