The basic principle is that there is a mountain of evidence of systemic abuse of children by government agencies already out there. We can build public acceptance for the existence of Pedogate by pointing to these mainstream sources. There are tons of people who respond to Pizzagate by saying, "That's crazy, we would know about something like that." What we want to do is reply, "Oh? Did you know about this? Or this? Or this?"

For example, Psychanaut found data supplied by the Children's Bureau going back more than a decade on various kinds of child abuse, including about 58,000 cases annually of child sexual abuse. What is very suspicious is the sub-section that relates to foster care. The Children's Bureau has consistently reported since at least 2005 that the rate of abuse among foster kids is 1/3rd to 1/4th as high as in the general population. It doesn't even break out sexual abuse of foster kids as a category. We know that this is crap. There are endless mainstream media stories specifically about sexual exploitation of foster kids, like these ones:

When it is ready, I will post it in this thread and everyone can critique it. Meanwhile, however, I need two things:

1) Links to stories about kids in foster care being abused.
