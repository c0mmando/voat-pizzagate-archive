As background, this is from the Manhattan Projects, by Jonathan Hickman with artist Nick Pitarra, from Image Comics. Basically, it is about an alternate word history focusing on scientists after the end of World War 2. In the second volume (published in trade paperback format in July of 2015, and individually before that), one of the characters is President Truman, who is portrayed as a member of an illuminati-style group of individuals that controlled the world. He has sacrificial orgies, and there is a scene of one linked above set in the early 1950s (Truman has the golden hat and robe, in the top-center). What struck me as really odd is the pizza guy in the bottom of the picture (it is in the blog link above, but separately here is an archive of just that image: http://archive.is/9QGZg ) who is carrying a Pizza box which says "Beware Pizza Place". Maybe it's nothing, but it predates all of the Pizzagate media, so it struck me as odd.
