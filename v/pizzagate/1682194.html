  • There are zero incentives for a CPS worker not to make false allegations (no consequences for being wrong).

  • Law enforcement sometimes goes out to force children in CPS custody to take drugs/medicine.

  • CPS gets $4,000 every time they remove a child from a home (on top of the lack of consequences for being wrong).
