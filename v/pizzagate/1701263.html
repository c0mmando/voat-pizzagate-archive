https://www.marinetraffic.com/en/ais/home/centerx:-71/centery:20/zoom:6

Marinetraffic is a LIVE map showing all registered, both smaller boats and cargo ships and their ports. Location information is based on radio signals, not gps. The map includes at least those vehicles required to inform about their routes, and additionally those with a supported radio in use. Privacy note: Cookies must be enabled (google can be left out, others are needed for vehicle visibility or you'll just see an empty map). Zoom in the shared link is set to cover the sea around Haiti.
