Quick how to:

Print at home:

  • Download the above pictures in one folder

  • Select all pictures in the folder

  • Right mouse click one the selected pictures and from the menu select "Print"

  • This will open a print preview of how the cards will be printed

The above templates are for 2 picture on one piece of photo paper (4x6 10x15) if you want to use A4 you need to select in the print preview the option for multiple picture on one page.

(Most print previews will give the option for printing multiple files on one page. for example 8 cards on one piece of normal a4 paper)

Other option is to download all pictures and put them on a USB drive and print them at a (self service) print shop. one print will result in two cards, and it should be cheap to print.
