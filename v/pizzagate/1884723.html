Not really sure how I stumbled upon this during my research, but I did.

Came across this site: http://www.enfantsdumande.org (it's in french)

What caught my eye was the swirly Pedo symbols in the logo. Which if you notice if you have the site open also is displayed on your web page Tab in the browser window. Not only is it a swirl, but I also noticed that it is blue, like the one displayed in the FBI Document . That peeked my curiosity. So I decided to look a bit further.

After translating the page it looks like an adoption agency based in Quebec.

Looked more into who runs the organization etc. on page - http://www.enfantsdumande.org/coordonnees/

Came across the name "Nathalie Tremblay"

Decided to punch it into Advangle, along with "Clinton"

Came across this PDF: http://www.cusointernational.org/docs/default-source/publications/annual-reviews/rapport-annuel-2012-2013.pdf?sfvrsn=8

It's the annual report for Cuso International.

On page 17 of the report you find - Nathalie Tremblay. Listed as one of the volunteers/organizations affiliated with Cuso International.

The section translated reads:

We would like to thank the Cuso International volunteers who In the areas of health and HIV / AIDS prevention in 2012-2013. They were Recruited in North America, Latin America, the Caribbean, Africa and Asia.

On page 21 you find the below (translated to english):

The Secretary of State American Hillary Rodham Clinton announced The World Forum on Diaspora held in Washington D.C., USAID's support for Partnership «Groups Diaspora in the service of Development »with Cuso International and Accenture.
