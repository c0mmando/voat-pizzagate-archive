There are also permits for construction. The latest permits are from 2014 but there is a large gap between 2003 and 2014. You'd think if there was a children's gym here at one point there would be construction. It also doesn't explain why this company was located at 115 as of a few months ago but now uses 114 (maybe they own both? The same contractor did work on both). https://www.buildzoom.com/property-info/8015-shoal-creek-blvd-unit-115-austin-tx-78757 Here are permits for suite 114, also a large gap between 1998 and 2013. https://www.buildzoom.com/property-info/8015-shoal-creek-blvd-unit-114-austin-tx-78757

Also, why were there only three recipients, why was the subject "proof", and why was it forwarded? If Elyse is this kid's mother wouldn't she be the original one to send this out? I think this merits more looking into especially with that sketchy address. What was in suite 115 on 08/26/07 since it definitely wasn't a children's gym and is there anything going on with these various title companies that use several different names at the same address?
